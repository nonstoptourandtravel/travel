Managing Customer Bookings

All bookings made through the website are automatically recorded and can be managed here. You can view, edit, confirm, or cancel bookings as needed.

Booking Workflow
  1. Customer submits booking form on website
  2. System automatically sends confirmation email to customer
  3. Admin receives notification of new booking
  4. Admin reviews and confirms booking details
  5. Payment process is initiated
  6. Upon payment, booking status is updated to "Confirmed"
  7. Travel vouchers are generated and sent to customer

Data Management Best Practices

Follow these guidelines to effectively manage and secure customer data while maximizing business insights.

Data Security
  • Limit admin panel access to authorized personnel only
  • Use strong, unique passwords and change them regularly
  • Enable two-factor authentication for admin accounts
  • Ensure all customer data is encrypted in storage and transit
  • Regularly backup customer database
Customer Engagement
  • Segment customers based on preferences and past bookings
  • Send personalized offers to returning customers
  • Track customer communication history
  • Collect and analyze customer feedback
  • Use data insights to develop new tour packages
